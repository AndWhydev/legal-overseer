# The AI Paralegal Built for Australian Law Firms

**Automates the admin. Keeps the lawyer in control. Your client data never leaves your server.**

---

### The three problems every small firm has

- **Admin eats billable hours.** Partners and lawyers lose chargeable time to intake, chasing documents, drafting, and deadline-wrangling.
- **Paralegal cost is enormous.** A full-time paralegal in Sydney runs $65,000–$85,000 a year — before super, leave, and supervision.
- **AI tools terrify them.** Confidentiality and ASCR duties make cloud AI a non-starter for client work.

### How Legal Overseer solves each

- **Admin → automated.** It runs intake, research, drafting, and deadline tracking so lawyers spend their time on judgement, not paperwork.
- **Paralegal cost → one annual licence.** It does the paralegal legwork for a fraction of a salary, with no leave, turnover, or training overhead.
- **Confidentiality → solved by design.** It installs on the firm's own server; privilege-sensitive text is redacted locally before any AI call, and nothing is stored off-site.

---

### The 5 things it does

1. **AustLII legal research** — with automatic citation verification (unverifiable authority is flagged, not hidden).
2. **Contract & document drafting** — letters, memos, contracts, court drafts, every one held behind a mandatory lawyer review gate.
3. **Limitation period monitoring** — calculates deadlines per matter type and state, and alerts the lawyer before time runs out.
4. **Client intake intelligence** — asks new clients the right questions for their matter type first, then hands the lawyer a structured brief before the first call.
5. **Monday morning briefing** — a per-lawyer summary of matters, deadlines, overdue items, and the review queue.

---

### The security story (this is the part that matters)

- **Installs on your firm's own server** — Windows or Linux, inside your network.
- **Client data never leaves your building** — no third-party cloud storage.
- **Not hosted in the US** — not exposed to the US CLOUD Act.
- **Compliant with ASCR rule 9.1** — confidentiality is preserved end to end.
- **Immutable audit trail** — every AI action is hash-chained and reviewable.
