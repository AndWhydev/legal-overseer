# Mission

Research BitBit's competitive landscape to validate the 'AI COO for small business' positioning hypothesis. Tiered approach: deep dive on OpenClaw, Claude Managed Agents, and 1 researcher-chosen direct competitor; quick scan of 5-10 adjacent players (Lindy.ai, Relevance AI, Dust.tt, MultiOn, etc.). Compare across three dimensions: (1) Operations — messaging integration, invoice handling, triage/routing, autonomy levels; (2) Tech/platform — AI model, API availability, deployment model, pricing, data privacy; (3) Market/ICP — target customer segment, pricing tier, go-to-market, funding stage. Output a competitive matrix plus positioning recommendation that validates 'AI COO for small business' as BitBit's differentiated niche or proposes a stronger alternative with evidence.
