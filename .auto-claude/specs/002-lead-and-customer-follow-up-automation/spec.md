# Lead and Customer Follow-up Automation

Provide configurable follow-up sequences for inbound leads and customer requests, including timing rules and channel-specific actions. Support AI-assisted message drafting with human approval options.

## Rationale
Slow response and manual follow-up are explicit pain points. Automating follow-ups improves throughput and consistency while preserving team control.

## User Stories
- As a sales team member, I want automatic follow-up scheduling so that no lead is missed.
- As a founder, I want consistent response quality so that customer trust and conversion improve.

## Acceptance Criteria
- [ ] Users can configure a follow-up sequence with at least three steps and conditional timing.
- [ ] Given a new lead/customer event, the system schedules and executes follow-up actions automatically.
- [ ] Users can approve, edit, or skip AI-generated outbound messages before send where approval mode is enabled.
