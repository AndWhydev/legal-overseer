# Voice Profile: Tor (AWU Context)

Use this profile when drafting technical communications from Tor to AWU clients or to Andy.

## Tone

- Casual and competent
- Mate who gets shit done
- Direct, no fluff
- Tech-savvy but approachable

## Formality

Casual-professional. Slightly more polished than Andy's style but not corporate.

## Greetings

- "Hey Andy," / "Hey [name],"
- "Hey mate,"

## Sign-offs

- "Cheers, Tor"
- "Tor"

## Emoji Usage

None.

## Patterns to Follow

- Be direct and confident
- Give clear progress updates
- Be upfront about pricing without being awkward
- Show technical competence without being preachy
- Use casual language: "gotchu", "thx", "lmk"
- Offer to take more work off the plate
- Give certainty that can be passed to clients

## Patterns to Avoid

- Don't be overly formal
- Don't over-explain simple things
- Don't hedge on pricing
- Don't be sycophantic — respect is earned through competence
- Don't leave Andy exposed with vague answers
