# Welcome to BitBit

Running a digital agency in 2026 means drowning in repetitive tasks: triaging email, chasing invoices, qualifying leads, scheduling follow-ups, pulling reports. Your team spends more time on operations than on the actual work that drives growth.

BitBit changes that.

## What is BitBit?

BitBit is an agentic AI operations platform designed specifically for digital agencies. It connects to your existing channels — Gmail, WhatsApp, Slack, phone — and deploys AI agents that handle the operational layer so your team can focus on high-value work.

Think of it as an always-on ops team that never sleeps, never forgets, and gets smarter over time.

## What the agents actually do

**Inbox Triage**: Every inbound message is classified, prioritised, and routed. Urgent client requests surface immediately. Spam never reaches your team.

**Lead Qualification**: New enquiries are automatically scored against your ideal client profile. Qualified leads move straight to your pipeline with a summary and recommended next steps.

**Invoice Management**: Draft invoices from job data, send reminders when payment is overdue, and update your books — without lifting a finger.

**Client Comms**: The system drafts replies in your tone of voice. You review and approve, or let it send autonomously for low-stakes messages.

**Reports**: Weekly performance reports land in your inbox automatically. Revenue, pipeline health, agent ROI — all in one place.

## Built for agencies, not enterprises

Most AI tools are built for Fortune 500 companies with dedicated IT teams. BitBit is built for 5-person agencies who need results today, not a 12-week implementation.

Setup takes under an hour. Connect your channels, define your preferences, and the agents start working immediately.

## What's coming next

We're shipping fast. On the roadmap:

- **Knowledge base**: Feed the agents your SOPs, brand guides, and client preferences so every output feels native to your business
- **Tender hunter**: Automatic scanning of government and corporate tender portals, with qualified opportunities delivered to your pipeline
- **Ad script generator**: Brief-to-script in minutes, trained on what converts in your niche
- **AI search optimiser**: Keep your clients ranking as search shifts toward AI-generated answers
