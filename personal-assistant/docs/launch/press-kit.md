### 150 Words
BitBit is an agentic AI operations platform that replaces the admin overhead crushing small businesses and agencies. Instead of another chatbot that waits for instructions, BitBit deploys a team of 10 specialist AI agents -- each owning an operational domain like lead qualification, invoicing, client communications, and proposal generation. Agents operate autonomously using confidence-based routing: routine tasks execute automatically, uncertain decisions get routed for approval, and edge cases escalate immediately.

What sets BitBit apart is its pre-compiled context engine. When new information arrives across any channel (email, WhatsApp, SMS, Slack), BitBit immediately builds structured understanding -- linking people, projects, conversations, and transactions into a persistent entity graph. Combined with Total Recall persistent memory, BitBit remembers every interaction across every channel permanently.

Built inside a real marketing agency (All Webbed Up, Australia) for 8 months. Every feature exists because a real operator needed it.

### 300 Words
BitBit is an agentic AI operations platform that deploys autonomous specialist agents to run business operations end-to-end. Founded by Tor Kay (technical) and Andy Taleb (commercial), BitBit was purpose-built inside Taleb's Australian marketing agency, All Webbed Up, as a live production system handling real client work from day one.

The platform addresses a universal problem: small business operators spend the majority of their time on operational admin -- email triage, invoice management, lead follow-up, proposal writing -- instead of the work their clients pay for. Traditional AI assistants compound this problem by requiring constant instruction and losing context between sessions.

BitBit takes a fundamentally different approach. The platform deploys 10 specialist AI agents, each owning a complete operational domain: Lead Swarm (qualification and response), Invoice Flow (generation, delivery, and payment chasing), Channel Triage (cross-channel message prioritization), Client Comms (voice-matched communication), Proposal Bot (scope and pricing automation), Ad Script Generator, Client Onboarding, AI Search Optimizer, Tender Hunter, and Sentry (background monitoring).

Agents operate autonomously using confidence-based routing. Actions above 80% confidence execute automatically. Uncertain decisions (50-80%) are routed for human approval via WhatsApp. Edge cases escalate immediately. This graduated trust model lets operators maintain control without micromanaging every decision.

The technical architecture centres on two innovations. The Context Baseplate pre-compiles understanding when information arrives, building a persistent entity graph that links people, projects, conversations, and transactions in real time. Total Recall provides permanent cross-channel conversational memory with three-tier compression, ensuring the system never loses context regardless of which channel a conversation spans.

During its 8-month deployment at All Webbed Up, BitBit reduced operator admin time from 25 hours per week to under 5, cut lead response time from hours to under 2 minutes, and eliminated lead loss entirely. The platform integrates with 15+ services including Gmail, WhatsApp, Xero, Stripe, and Google Calendar.

## Key Features

- **10 Specialist Agents**: Lead Swarm, Invoice Flow, Channel Triage, Client Comms, Proposal Bot, Ad Script Generator, Client Onboarding, AI Search Optimizer, Tender Hunter, Sentry. Activate only the agents your business needs.

- **Confidence-Based Action Routing**: Agents autonomously execute high-confidence tasks, request approval for uncertain decisions via WhatsApp, and immediately escalate edge cases. Graduated trust model.

- **Context Baseplate**: Pre-compiled understanding engine. Processes incoming information in real time to build a persistent entity graph linking people, projects, conversations, and transactions across all channels.

- **Total Recall Memory**: Three-tier conversation compression maintains full conversational context permanently. Last 10 turns verbatim, 11-30 compressed, 31+ distilled to key facts (commitments, deadlines, financial amounts).

- **Cross-Channel Identity Resolution**: One conversation thread per person regardless of channel. WhatsApp, email, SMS, Slack -- all mapped to a single identity with continuous context.

- **Smart Tool Orchestration**: Fast planner selects relevant tools per request from 25+ available tools. 90-95% KV cache hit rate. Keeps responses fast and cost-efficient.

- **Multi-Model Routing**: Haiku for classification (70% of calls), Sonnet for drafting (25%), Opus for strategy (5%), Gemini Flash for background monitoring. Cost-optimised by complexity.

- **15+ Integrations**: Gmail, WhatsApp, Xero, Stripe, Google Calendar, Slack, ClickUp, WordPress, Google Analytics, and more. Single-click connection via unified grid.

- **Approval Workflows**: All outbound communications can be routed through approval queues during initial deployment. Conversational approval ("yep, send it") via any channel.

- **Per-Agent Pricing**: No per-seat fees. No usage-based surprises. Activate the agents your business needs. Scale by adding agents, not adding seats.
