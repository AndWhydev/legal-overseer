# BitBit -- Product Hunt Listing

## Tagline (60 chars)
Your AI operations team that remembers everything.

## Short Description (260 chars)
BitBit deploys autonomous AI agents that actually run your business ops. It reads your emails, sends invoices, qualifies leads, writes proposals, and follows up with clients -- across every channel. It remembers everything and acts without being asked.

## Full Description

### The problem every operator knows too well

You started a business to do work you're good at. Instead, you spend half your day on admin. Email triage, invoice chasing, lead follow-up, proposal writing, client updates -- spread across Gmail, WhatsApp, Slack, Xero, and a dozen other tools. Context lives everywhere and nowhere. Leads slip through cracks. Invoices go out late. You become the bottleneck in your own company.

Most "AI assistants" make this worse, not better. They wait for instructions. They need babysitting. They forget everything the moment the conversation ends. You end up managing the AI instead of the AI managing your work.

### BitBit is different

BitBit is not a chatbot. It is a team of specialist AI agents that operate your business autonomously. Each agent owns a domain -- leads, invoicing, communications, proposals, tenders -- and works within policies you define. When a new email arrives, BitBit has already figured out who sent it, what it relates to, and whether it needs your attention. That understanding was built before you asked.

Connect your services (Gmail, WhatsApp, Xero, Stripe, Calendar -- 15+ integrations), and BitBit builds a living model of your world. It knows your clients by name, remembers that Dave owes you money, and links his Tuesday email to Steve's project. Every interaction across every channel feeds into one persistent memory that never forgets.

### What makes it work

- **10 specialist agents**: Lead Swarm, Invoice Flow, Channel Triage, Client Comms, Proposal Bot, Ad Script Generator, Client Onboarding, AI Search Optimizer, Tender Hunter, and Sentry (background monitoring). Pick the ones you need.
- **Confidence-based routing**: High confidence actions execute automatically. Uncertain calls get routed for your approval via WhatsApp. Edge cases escalate immediately. You stay in control without micromanaging.
- **Total Recall memory**: Three-tier conversation compression keeps full context across every channel. BitBit remembers commitments, deadlines, financial amounts, and relationship history -- permanently.
- **Context Baseplate**: Not typical search-and-retrieve. BitBit compiles understanding when new information arrives, building an entity graph with relationships, patterns, and active threads in real time.
- **Smart tool orchestration**: A fast planner selects only relevant tools per request from 25+ available, keeping responses fast and costs low (90-95% cache hit rate).
- **Cross-channel identity**: One conversation thread per person, whether they message you on WhatsApp, email, SMS, or Slack. No more fragmented context.

### Built for operators, not IT departments

BitBit was built inside a real marketing agency (All Webbed Up) handling real client work. Every feature exists because a real operator needed it. Message BitBit from a job site: "Hey Bit, invoice Dave for the kitchen job." It knows who Dave is, what the job cost, and sends a branded PDF invoice -- no forms, no clicks.

Per-agent pricing. No per-seat fees. No usage surprises. Your BitBit grows with your business.
