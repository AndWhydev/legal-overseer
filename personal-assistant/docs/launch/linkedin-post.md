# BitBit -- LinkedIn Launch Post

---

I spent 8 months building an AI system inside a real marketing agency. Not as a side project. As the actual operations layer running real client work.

Today we are launching BitBit publicly.

The problem: my business partner Andy runs All Webbed Up, a digital agency in Australia. He was spending 25+ hours a week on operations -- email triage, invoice chasing, lead follow-up, proposal writing. Across Gmail, WhatsApp, Xero, Slack, and half a dozen other tools. Context lived everywhere and nowhere. Leads fell through cracks. Invoices went out late. He became the bottleneck in his own company.

Most AI tools would not fix this. Chatbots wait for instructions. They forget everything between sessions. You end up managing the AI instead of the AI managing the work.

So we built something different.

BitBit deploys a team of specialist AI agents that own entire domains of your business. Lead qualification. Invoicing. Client communications. Proposals. Tender hunting. Each agent operates autonomously within policies you define, using confidence-based routing -- high confidence actions just happen, uncertain calls get your approval via WhatsApp, edge cases escalate immediately.

The technical differentiator: BitBit compiles understanding when new information arrives, not when you ask a question. It builds a persistent entity graph linking people, projects, conversations, and transactions across every channel. By the time you ask "what's happening with the Henderson project?", BitBit has already connected the emails, the invoice, the WhatsApp thread, and the calendar entries.

What is built and shipping today:
-- 10 specialist agents (pick the ones you need)
-- 15+ service integrations (Gmail, WhatsApp, Xero, Stripe, Calendar, and more)
-- Cross-channel identity resolution (one conversation per person, any channel)
-- Total Recall persistent memory (three-tier compression, never forgets)
-- Confidence-based action routing with approval workflows
-- Per-agent pricing (no per-seat fees)

Early results from the AWU deployment: lead response time went from hours to under 2 minutes. Zero leads lost. Invoices out same-day instead of days later. Andy's ops time dropped from 25 hours to under 5 hours per week.

We are taking signups now at bitbit.chat.

If you run a service business, agency, or trades company and you are drowning in admin -- this was built for you. Literally. Every feature exists because a real operator needed it.
