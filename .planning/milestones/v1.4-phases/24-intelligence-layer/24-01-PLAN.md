# Plan 24-01: Revenue Radar + Client Health Score

## Goal
Revenue Radar identifies upsell opportunities and flags stale clients. Client Health Score computes per-client 0-100 score from real signals.

## Tasks
1. **revenue-radar.ts** — `src/lib/intelligence/revenue-radar.ts`: analyzeRevenueOpportunities(supabase, orgId). Scans client history for upsell, stale clients, pricing gaps. Caches in bi_snapshots.
2. **client-health.ts** — `src/lib/intelligence/client-health.ts`: computeClientHealth(supabase, orgId). Per-client score from response times, payments, project progress, communication. Returns ClientHealthScore[].
3. **Tests** — `src/lib/intelligence/__tests__/intelligence.test.ts`: revenue radar + client health scoring.
