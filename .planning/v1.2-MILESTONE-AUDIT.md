### 1. WhatsApp QR Pairing Flow

**Break point:** `connect-modal.tsx` `WhatsAppQRPanel.handleInitiate()` line ~167
**Affected:** OAUTH-03, CHAN-03

### 2. Scheduled Channel Sync with Dedup
**Steps:** Vercel cron fires → channel-sync pulls via `getAllAdapters()` directly → **BREAK** → No dedup check → No latency instrumentation → No classification retry

**Affected:** CHAN-04, CHAN-05
