# What's Built

Every platform on the market solves one problem. Lindy builds workflows. Superhuman handles email. Motion schedules your calendar. Jasper writes copy. None of them talk to each other.

BitBit is one system that owns your entire business operation. It reads every channel, knows every contact, handles invoicing, triages messages, writes proposals, and works while you sleep. This page covers what's live today.

## Core Capabilities

### Connections

13 channels wired. Gmail, Outlook, WhatsApp, Android Messages, iMessage, SMS, Slack, Asana, Calendly, Stripe, Xero, Facebook Messenger, Instagram. Connect with a click or a QR scan. iMessage connects through a secure sign in experience.

Messaging bridges run per user instances on your actual accounts. Not API wrappers. Your real conversations, your real identity.

### Agent Engine

Every request goes through a Think, Act, Observe, Reflect loop with no iteration cap. The model decides when it's done.

A fast classifier reads your intent and selects only the relevant tools (5 to 12 out of 26 total). This keeps responses fast and costs low with 90%+ cache hit rates.

Three tier confidence routing controls what happens autonomously:
- High confidence: execute and report
- Medium confidence: queue for your approval
- Low confidence: ask you directly

### Autonomous Roles

Six specialist roles that own entire domains. Not chatbot responses. Continuous autonomous operation.

**Finance** handles invoicing, collections, cash flow tracking, and payment timing. It knows which clients pay late.

**Comms** triages every channel, drafts responses that match each conversation's tone, and manages follow ups automatically.

**Sales** qualifies leads, manages your pipeline, generates proposals from meeting transcripts, and runs nurture sequences.

**Ad Scripts** generates platform adapted copy for Meta, Google, and LinkedIn with daily budget caps.

**SEO and Tender Hunter** runs audits, recommends content, validates schemas, finds tenders, checks eligibility, and generates responses. The tender capability is the premium play.

**Content Creator** writes blog posts, social content, and manages a content calendar with scheduling.

**Builder** generates websites through conversation with WordPress and Elementor integration. Preview in a sandbox, deploy from chat.

Each role runs at one of three autonomy levels: Observer (watch only), Co pilot (draft and approve), or Autopilot (act and report). Tools auto graduate from Co pilot to Autopilot based on your approval history.

### Knowledge Graph

BitBit builds a graph of every person, company, project, and invoice it encounters. Fact extraction fires on every conversation. When you say "invoice Sezer for the White House RE work," BitBit already knows who Sezer is, what the work was, the rate, and whether it's been invoiced.

### Memory System

Six layer memory with sleep consolidation. Recent conversations are kept verbatim. Older ones compress to key facts: commitments, decisions, deadlines, amounts. A background process strengthens important connections during low activity. Cross channel identity resolution ties your WhatsApp, email, SMS, and Slack contacts together.

### Proactive Intelligence

Four always on modules that compute from real data with minimum thresholds:

- **Revenue Radar** identifies upsell opportunities from spending patterns
- **Client Health Score** rates every relationship 0 to 100 from engagement signals
- **Cash Flow Prophet** projects finances forward with confidence intervals
- **Capacity Oracle** warns before you overcommit

### Billing

Four tiers from $10/mo (core agents) to $2,000/mo (tender hunting). 30 day free trial. Stripe checkout with customer portal.

### Mobile

React Native app with push notifications, voice input via push to talk, offline queue that syncs when reconnected, and swipeable approvals.
