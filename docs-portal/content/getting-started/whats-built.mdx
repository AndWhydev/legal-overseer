# What's Built

Every AI tool on the market does one thing. Superhuman triages your email. Motion manages your calendar. Jasper writes your blog posts. Lindy chains together workflows. They all live in separate tabs, none of them talk to each other, and the moment you need something across two of them, you're back to copy paste.

BitBit replaces the lot. One system that reads every channel, knows every contact, handles your invoicing, triages your messages, writes your proposals, and keeps working after you close your laptop.

|---|---|---|

| Calendar management | Motion ($34/mo, calendar only) | Built in, cross referenced with contacts and active projects |
| Sales outreach | Artisan Ava ($900+/mo, outbound only) | Built in with lead qualification, proposals from meeting transcripts, nurture sequences |
| Content creation | Jasper ($49/mo, writing only) | Built in with blog, social, ad scripts adapted per platform |
| Customer support | Intercom Fin ($0.99/resolution, reactive) | Built in, proactive not reactive |
| CRM and pipeline | HubSpot ($800+/mo, locked ecosystem) | Built in, learns from real conversations instead of manual data entry |
| Workflow automation | Lindy ($50/mo, visual builder) | Built in, natural language rules, no drag and drop required |

<Tip>
The real difference is not features. It is that BitBit thinks ahead. When a new email arrives, BitBit already knows who sent it, what project it connects to, whether there is an outstanding invoice, and whether it actually needs your attention.
</Tip>

## Agent engine

Every request runs through a Think, Act, Observe, Reflect loop. No iteration cap. The model decides when the job is done.

A fast classifier picks only the tools that matter for each request (5 to 12 out of 26 total), which keeps responses fast and costs low with 90%+ cache reuse.

**Builder role** generates websites through conversation with WordPress and Elementor integration. Preview in a sandbox, deploy from chat.

Each role runs at one of three levels you control:

| Level | Behaviour |
|---|---|
| Observer | Watches and reports. Takes no action. |
| Co pilot | Drafts and proposes. You approve before anything happens. |
| Autopilot | Acts on its own. Reports after. Escalates when uncertain. |

Tools auto graduate from Co pilot to Autopilot based on your approval history. Approve the same type of action enough times and BitBit stops asking.

### Proactive intelligence

Four modules that run continuously on real data:

| Module | What it does |
|---|---|
| Revenue Radar | Spots upsell opportunities from customer spending patterns |
| Client Health Score | Rates every relationship 0 to 100 from real engagement signals |
| Cash Flow Prophet | Projects your finances forward with confidence intervals |
| Capacity Oracle | Warns before you take on more than you can handle |

<Note>
All four modules enforce minimum data thresholds. If the data is not sufficient to make a reliable prediction, BitBit will not show one.
</Note>
